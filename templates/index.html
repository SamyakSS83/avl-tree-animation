AVL Tree Rotations Demonstrations

This application demonstrates the various rotations in AVL trees during insertion and deletion operations. AVL trees are self-balancing binary search trees where the height difference between the left and right subtrees (balance factor) for any node is at most 1.

LL Case: When the left subtree becomes too heavy on the left side.

RR Case: When the right subtree becomes too heavy on the right side.

LR Case: When the left subtree becomes too heavy on its right side.

RL Case: When the right subtree becomes too heavy on its left side.
